<Section icon={Target} title="Upgrade and zone timing">

Upgrades should solve the exact problem that stopped the last run. If zombies are reaching the base, damage or coverage usually deserves the next spend. If waves are already stable, zones become the better long-term move because they support broader progression.

| Focus | Buy When | Skip When | Result |
|---|---|---|---|
| Core damage | Zombies survive too long | The real issue is placement | Faster wave clears |
| Coverage and placement | Enemies slip through open lanes | Raw damage is still too low | Fewer leaks |
| Roll quality setup | Income is banked and luck bonuses are active | The current wave is already failing | Better future pulls |
| Zone unlock | Your current defense clears calmly | The next zone would overextend the run | Safer progression |

The cleanest spending order is simple: fix survival, improve roll value, then expand. That order keeps income from scattering across too many menus.

<Callout type="success" title="Spend Order">
Upgrade the bottleneck first, not every option at once. One strong fix usually creates more progress than three small purchases.
</Callout>

</Section>
