The upgrade path in **Roll to Defend** works best when every purchase solves one problem. That keeps your income efficient and prevents the classic trap of buying a little bit of everything, which feels active but often slows progress.

| Bottleneck | Best Spend | Buy When | Hold Off When |
|---|---|---|---|

| Coverage | Improve lane coverage | A strong unit leaves gaps | Damage is already too low |

| Zone push | Unlock the next area | Current waves clear cleanly | You need emergency spending to survive |
| Economy | Reinvest income | You are already stable | You still need a combat fix |

A good rule is simple: if the current setup cannot defend itself calmly, it is not time to expand. A stronger zone only helps when the team you already built can survive the pressure after the unlock.
